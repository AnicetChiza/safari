About us

Rwanda is a country of thousand hills. The Asher Safari is a tour operations company offering different touristic services. We do adventure tours as our first purpose, tour packages, community based activities, car and boat transfers, tour guiding and porter services, boat tours and also do hotel reservations/booking services. We provide memorable services to explore and discover Rwanda to exceptional people at affordable prices everyone can afford.
